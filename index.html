Founded in 2013, Lighthouse Labs is a modern, immersive education approach for web and mobile software development. Our hands-on and accelerated coding bootcamps connect passionate, driven people to careers as software developers. Through a unique approach to education that is both data and community driven, we've been successful in helping graduates achieve their ambitious career goals. In just two years, we've graduated over 450 students and maintain over 95% employment rates for our job seeking alumni.

Welcome

Lighthouse Labs was created in 2013 by a team of software developers with a passion for code, mentorship, and education. Our mission: to continuously find the best ways to train the next generation of developers and transform the way tech education is delivered. Five years later, we have introduced over 20,000 Canadians to the practice of coding and launched 1,000+ graduates into careers as professional developers. With the support of a brilliant team of instructors and mentors who view coding as the ultimate craft, we continue to empower students, launch careers, and contribute to the incredible growth of Canada's tech industry.

Our back-end staff keep the ship afloat. We're a group of quirky individuals who are committed to making the Lighthouse Labs student experience and quality of education second to none. We love dogs, rubber ducks, gifs, and bake offs.
